For School Administrators and Nurses

Bring Pads for Peers to Your School

Pads for Peers helps schools provide free menstrual care products to students through a simple, discreet monthly Comfort Box program—with minimal effort required from staff.

Express Interest

Helping students access what they need, while keeping the process easy for schools

In many schools, students who need menstrual products often have to rely on informal solutions—asking the nurse, going to the office, or trying to manage without what they need. That can be uncomfortable for students and inconsistent for staff. Pads for Peers was created to make menstrual care products more accessible to students who need them, while also helping schools offer support in a way that is organized, discreet, and easy to manage.

What We Do

Comfort Box Program

Our core program is the Comfort Box Program. Through this program, participating students receive free monthly menstrual care products in a way that protects privacy and keeps pickup simple. Students enroll privately and pick up their kits at school in a way that is discreet and predictable.

How It Works

1

Your School Expresses Interest

A school administrator, nurse, or staff member submits the interest form below. This only takes 2 minutes.

2

We Help Your School Get Set Up

We work with you to onboard your school, share materials, posters, and all material you will need to make this a success at your school.

3

Students Sign Up Privately

Students scan the QR code on the posters to discretely enroll and choose the products they need. Students without access to a mobile device can sign up using a link.

4

Simple Pickup at School

We deliver Comfort Boxes every month to the school. Students pick up discreetly from a designated location such as the front office or nurse's office.

What the School Needs to Do

We understand that school staff has plenty to do. So we designed Pads for Peers to be easy for schools to support. In most cases, the school only needs to provide a designated student pickup location, display student posters in appropriate places, and hand boxes to students when they come to pick them up.

  • No need to run a new program
  • No purchasing of products
  • No complicated inventory process
  • No heavy administrative burden

Benefits to Students and Schools

  • Reliable access to menstrual care products
  • A more private and comfortable way for students to get support
  • Reduced ad-hoc requests handled by staff
  • Consistent support system for students
  • Supports student well-being and school participation
  • Advances a more open and supportive culture around periods

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to the school?

No. Pads for Peers is designed to provide support to students without cost to the school.

How much work is involved for school staff?

Very little. In most cases, staff only need to provide a pickup location and hand boxes to students when they come to pick them up.

Do students sign up publicly?

No. The process is designed to be private and discreet.

Do we need to manage inventory or orders?

The goal of the program is to keep logistics as simple as possible for schools and minimize school-side overhead.
