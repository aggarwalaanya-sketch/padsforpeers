What We Do

Comfort Box Program

Our Comfort Box is a customizable monthly subscription box that lets participants choose the period products they need. We provide free products while keeping everything confidential through a secure QR code system for discreet pickup and delivery. Currently, over 60 students receive Comfort Boxes, and we're working to expand the program's impact.

Providing Period Education

Breaking the stigma and misinformation around menstruation starts with education. Many young students don't fully understand what it means to have a period, so we focus on open, discussion-based conversations rather than traditional classroom instruction. This approach helps students feel more comfortable sharing, asking questions, and learning about menstrual health.

The Breaking Barriers Blog

Our blog helps advance our mission to raise awareness and break the stigma around periods. We share articles and personal stories on menstrual health, period poverty, and women's wellness to promote understanding and normalize conversations about menstruation. By highlighting diverse perspectives and evidence-based information, we aim to empower individuals with knowledge and build a more open, supportive culture around periods.
